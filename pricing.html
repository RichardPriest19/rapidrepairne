Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical repair take?

Minor scratches can be completed same-day, while dent repairs typically take 24-48 hours. Full resprays require 3-7 days depending on the vehicle size and condition.

Do you match the exact paint color?

Yes! We use advanced colour-matching technology to ensure a perfect match with your vehicle's original paint, accounting for factors like age and sun exposure.

Is there a warranty on your work?

All our repairs come with a quality guarantee. Standard repairs include a 12-month warranty, while full resprays include an extended warranty.

Can I get a quote before bringing my vehicle in?

Absolutely! Contact us with photos and details of the damage, and we'll provide a preliminary quote. For the most accurate estimate, we recommend a free in-person inspection.
